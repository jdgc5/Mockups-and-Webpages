LUXURY TRAIN TOUR - 7 DAYS

DAY 1

Start your Adventure

Palace on Wheels journey through the pages of history shall begin on Wednesday – the day of the week when the train officially runs. On the beginning of the day, we shall welcome you aboard with true and ethnic Rajasthani welcome and a warm drink. On boarding the train, enjoy the beauty of the train from inside and feel like a Royal yourself!

DAY 2

Jaipur

The Pink City of Rajasthan is overnight. The second day of the tour shall see you stepping down in Jaipur. Established by Maharajah Sawai Jai Singh II, it is one of the most beautiful cities of Rajasthan. So, soon after you finish your breakfast on board, we will take you around Hawa Mahal, Amber Fort, City Palace. Dinner shall be served on board.

DAY 3

Sawai Madhopur

On the 3rd day of the travel we shall reach Sawai Madhopur which is famous for Ranthambore National Park. It was founded by Maharajah Sawai Madho Singh I. It is also considered one of the best place in India to see tigers! Chittorgarh is our next destination Chittorgarh is a place where history resounds itself. Chittorgarh is famous for its Fort which is the largest in India.

DAY 4

Udaipur (City Of Lake)

One of the most enduring places in Rajasthan is Udaipur – with lakes all around! It’s a place which reminisces the Mewar history in the form of its Historical Buildings. See here Jag Niwas Palace or the Lake Palace.

DAY 5

Jaisalmer (The Golden City)

Called 'The Land of Forts and Havelis' is where sand dance to the waves of winds and yellow sand-stone forts. Established by Maharana Rawal Jaisal; this city boasts of the royalty of northern India. Jaisalmer city hosts an annual Desert Festival which attracts many tourists every year.

DAY 6

Jodhpur (The Blue City)

Next destination on Palace on Wheels is Jodhpur – the land of the Sun! Enclosed in high walls of fort – its famous for its summers and Umaid Palace – the Royal Residence of present Prince of Rajasthan.

DAY 7

Bharatpur / Agra

Bharatpur is where the Keoladeo Ghana National Park is located. Home to over 230 species of migratory birds including rare Siberian Crane, it is considered UNESCO WORLD HERITAGE SITE. We shall move on from here to Agra – to see the TAJ MAHAL – one of the SEVEN WONDERS OF THE WORLD. On reaching here immerse yourself in the beauty of this timeless symbol of eternal love.

ROUTE HIGHLIGHTS

Hawa Mahal

Hawa Mahal, or the Palace of Winds, stands as an iconic architectural gem in Jaipur, India. Adorned with 953 intricately designed windows, this five-story palace allowed royal women to observe the city's life while maintaining privacy. Built of red and pink sandstone, it remains a testament to Jaipur's rich heritage and architectural ingenuity.

Udaipor Palace

The City Palace in Udaipur is a magnificent blend of Rajasthani and Mughal architecture, set against the backdrop of Lake Pichola. It houses museums, royal chambers, and a crystal gallery, showcasing the grandeur of Udaipur's history and royalty.

Jaisalmer Fort

Jaisalmer Fort, also called the "Golden Fort", stands tall in the Thar Desert. Its stunning golden walls showcase intricate designs and balconies, embodying Rajasthani craftsmanship. This living fort houses palaces, temples, and narrow pathways, encapsulating a rich historical legacy within its captivating architecture.

INDIA GOLDEN TRIANGLE TOUR - 7 DAYS

DAY 1

Welcome - New Delhi

Upon arrival at India Gandhi International Airport, you will be met by our representative and escorted to your hotel. After a brief introduction to your India tour, you will check into your hotel and have some free time on your own to adjust to the new time zone.

DAY 2

Heritage Sightseeing of Delhi

Explore Old Delhi's Jama Masjid and Chandni Chowk before heading to New Delhi for Qutub Minar, Gandhi Smriti, and Humayun's Tomb. Drive by iconic landmarks like India Gate, the President's House, and Parliament House, concluding the day at the vibrant Laxmi Narayan Temple for a glimpse into local religion.

DAY 3

Delhi - Agra

Travel to Agra in a private A/C car, enjoying scenic stops along the way for photos or a coffee break. Upon reaching Agra, explore the enchanting Mehtab Bagh, also known as the Moonlight Garden, offering a mesmerizing preview of the Taj Mahal against the backdrop of the setting sun.

DAY 4

Timeless Taj Mahal, Agra

This morning takes you early to the breathtaking Taj Mahal to avoid the crowds and heat in the daytime. Just immerse yourself in its amazing architecture, history, and beauty. Continue and discover the palatial Royal Pavilions at the red sandstone Agra Fort for a glimpse of the royal Mughal lifestyle. After that, end your exploration at Itmad-ud-daulah (Baby Taj).

DAY 5

Turning to Jaipur

Drive west to Jaipur this morning. En-route, roam around the former royal city of Fatehpur Sikri (about 1.5 hours' drive) en-route. The red-sandstone palace walls are at the most atmospheric and photogenic near sunset. Proceed to Jaipur (about 3.5 hours' drive) to check in and stay overnight.

DAY 6

Historical Beauty of Jaipur

Drive back to Jaipur and visit Nahargarh Fort on the way. It opens up a grand panorama of the surrounding countryside. Stop at the city landmark: the pink Hawa Mahal for a picture opportunity. Continue to visit the City Palace Museum, a royal palace with seven storey, and Jantar Mantar. This wonderful observatory has attracted many mathematicians and astronomers around the world. Drive through the rose pink residential and business areas before returning to your hotel.

DAY 7

Last Glance , Departure

After breakfast, your private driver will escort you to Delhi (about 5 hours). Upon arrival, you may take advantage of some time for a last minute gift shopping before our representative escorts you to the airport for your return flight.

ROUTE HIGHLIGHTS

Taj Mahal

A mesmerizing testament to undying love, the Taj Mahal, an ivory-white marble mausoleum, embodies sheer architectural perfection. Adorned with intricate calligraphy and delicate carvings, this UNESCO World Heritage Site shines during sunrise and sunset, casting a golden aura over its symmetrical beauty. Set amidst serene gardens and a reflecting pool, its ethereal charm captivates all who visit.

Amber Fort

Step into the Rajput era at the Amber Fort, a majestic fortress boasting exquisite architecture adorned with detailed frescoes, mirror work, and marble inlays. The legendary Sheesh Mahal within, a palace adorned with thousands of mirrors, dazzles with its intricate craftsmanship. Perched on a hill, the fort offers sweeping views of the Aravalli Range and Maota Lake, immersing visitors in royal splendor and panoramic vistas.

Humayun's Tomb

An architectural gem from the Mughal era, Humayun's Tomb stands as a historic marvel. Constructed with red sandstone and intricate marble detailing, this UNESCO World Heritage Site mirrors the grandeur of the Taj Mahal. Surrounded by lush gardens, the tomb's symmetrical design and serene ambiance invite contemplation, honoring its significance as a pivotal point in Mughal architectural evolution.

SOUTH INDIA JEWELS - 5 DAYS

DAY 1

Mahabalipuram

Upon arrival at Chennai International Airport, transfer to Mahabalipuram. Check-in at the hotel and spend the afternoon exploring the city's ancient rock caves, temples, and the captivating beachfront.

DAY 2

Kanchipuram Excursion

Embark on a day trip to Kanchipuram, renowned as the city of a thousand temples. Discover the historic Pallava capital, visit notable temples, and explore its rich silk heritage. Return to Mahabalipuram to unwind and enjoy the local ambiance.

DAY 3

Visit Pondicherry

Travel to Pondicherry, making a stop at the visionary township of Auroville. Explore the serene Sri Aurobindo Ashram and delve into the charming streets of Pondicherry, resonating with French colonial influence, spirituality, and cultural diversity.

DAY 4

Kumbakonam & Chidambaram

Journey to Kumbakonam while en route visiting Chidambaram, a significant site for Shiva devotees. Explore the sacred temples, including Darasuram, and soak in the spiritual aura of this historical town nestled amidst rivers.

DAY 5

Tanjore Temple, Trichy, and Chettinad

Discover the architectural marvel of Tanjore's Brihadeshwara Temple dedicated to Lord Shiva. Visit Trichy's iconic Srirangam Temple and the towering rock fort. Conclude the day by heading to Chettinadu, immersing in its unique cultural heritage and architectural splendor.

ROUTE HIGHLIGHTS

Mamallapuram

Known for its ancient rock-cut temples, cave sanctuaries, and the iconic Shore Temple by the sea, Mahabalipuram offers a glimpse into Pallava architecture and UNESCO-listed heritage sites.

Kanchipuram

Revered as the "City of Thousand Temples," Kanchipuram boasts a wealth of impressive Hindu temples, highlighting Dravidian architecture and silk weaving traditions.

Tanjore

The Brihadeshwara Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, stands as a marvel of Chola architecture in Tanjore. This grand temple dedicated to Lord Shiva is renowned for its architectural brilliance and cultural significance.
