Visit our Facilities

Zoo facilities are the cornerstone of a comprehensive and unforgettable visitor experience.

They form a multifaceted ensemble of essential components that tend to the practical requirements of guests while simultaneously deepening their appreciation of wildlife and the intricate tapestry of biodiversity.

These facilities, which comprise the captivating animal habitats, enticing gift shops, delectable dining establishments, and convenient restroom facilities, harmoniously blend to elevate a zoo visit into an all-encompassing, educational, and enjoyable expedition that caters to individuals of all age groups.

Exploring our Habitats

We invite you on a journey through the diverse and captivating worlds of our zoo's animal residents.

Each habitat is carefully designed to replicate the natural environments of the incredible animals that call it home. As you explore these unique ecosystems, you'll gain a deeper understanding of the biodiversity of our planet and the importance of conservation.

Our commitment to creating these habitats extends beyond providing a safe haven for our animals; it's a dedication to educating and inspiring our visitors about the wonders of the natural world.

Rainforest Habitat

The rainforest habitat at our zoo is a captivating and immersive experience that transports you into the heart of one of the Earth's most biologically diverse ecosystems.

The canopy overhead filters sunlight to create a mesmerizing interplay of light and shadow. Playful monkeysswing from branch to branch, colorful birds dart through the foliage, and a rich tapestry of plants and animals thrives in this verdant world.

Glistening streams wind through the undergrowth, providing essential water sources for the habitat's inhabitants. From the vibrant poison dart frogs to the majestic jaguards, the rainforest habitat offers a close encounter with a stunning array of species, each playing a unique role in the complex web of life in this tropical wonderland.

Savannah Habitat

Welcome to the expansive and open landscapes of the savannah, a habitat that faithfully recreates the sweeping vistas of the African plains. Here, you'll step into a captivating world where graceful giraffes, mighty lions, and an assortment of herbivores roam freely across the golden terrain.

As you venture into this immersive environment, you'll be immediately struck by the sense of vastness that characterizes the savannah. The horizon seems to stretch endlessly, meeting the boundless sky, and the warm African sun bathes the landscape in a gentle, golden glow.

Elegant giraffes can be seen gracefully foraging on the treetops, their long necks reaching for tender leaves. Majestic lions, the undisputed rulers of the savannah, may be found resting beneath the shade of acacia trees, ever-watchful for signs of potential prey.

Aquatic Habitat

Prepare to be enchanted as you dive into the captivating world of aquatic wonders, where the waters come alive with a kaleidoscope of shimmering fish in every hue of the rainbow.

Graceful sea turtles glide effortlessly through the crystal-clear depths, their ancient wisdom evident in every tranquil movement. Playful otters dart and frolic, bringing a sense of childlike joy to their underwater ballet. This aquatic realm isn't just a visual spectacle; it's a profound experience that immerses you in the wonders of marine life.

As you explore this underwater world, you'll witness vibrant coral reefs teeming with life, where intricate ecosystems have evolved.

Desert Habitat

In this harsh environment, a remarkable cast of creatures has adapted and thrives against all odds. Desert foxes, with their sandy coats and sharp senses, silently traverse the dunes in search of sustenance.

Majestic camels, the "ships of the desert," elegantly traverse the arid terrain, their resilience a testament to their enduring importance in these harsh landscapes. Elusive scorpions, masters of survival, patrol the night, their armored bodies designed to withstand the scorching days and freezing nights.

Our desert habitat is a living testament to the remarkable strategies that life has developed to endure some of the world's most extreme environments. It's an environment where every inhabitant, from the smallest insect to the largest mammal, is an embodiment of nature's incredible adaptability.

Visitor-Friendly Amenities

At our zoo, your comfort and convenience are paramount, and we offer a range of visitor-friendly amenities to enhance your experience.

Our enticing gift shop is a treasure trove of souvenirs, from animal-themed merchandise to eco-friendly products, and your purchase directly supports our conservation efforts. Clean and well-maintained restroom facilities, equipped with family-friendly amenities, are conveniently located throughout the zoo, ensuring a comfortable visit for all.

For those who prefer to dine amidst the natural beauty of the zoo, our picnic facilities provide a relaxing space to enjoy your own packed meal. Additionally, our on-site restaurants offer diverse culinary options, from leisurely meals to quick snacks, allowing you to refuel during your visit. For young adventurers, our carousel provides a delightful experience that adds an extra layer of enjoyment to your day at the zoo.
