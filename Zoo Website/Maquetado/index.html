Welcome to Wild Adventure Granada

Nestled in Granada's breathtaking landscapes, our park is a haven for wildlife enthusiasts and conservationists. our dedicated team is on a mission to inspire, educate, and protect.

Explore habitats that mirror the wild, from majestic big cats to vibrant avian species. But we're more than a park; we're a community. Beyond our borders, we collaborate with local communities and global organizations to safeguard endangered species and ecosystems.

At Wild Adventure Granada, we're not just about observing nature; we're about experiencing it. Join us for an immersive journey, where you'll gain a profound understanding of our planet's delicate balance. It's more than a visit; it's a promise of adventure, education, and a brighter future for our natural world.

Our Story

2005

A Vision Takes Root

In 2005, the seed of Wild Adventure Granada was planted. It all began with a passionate group of wildlife enthusiasts who dreamed of creating a haven for nature lovers and a sanctuary for endangered species.

2008

Conservation Commitment

By 2008, our commitment to conservation was firmly established. We began collaborating with local conservation organizations, laying the groundwork for our future efforts in protecting biodiversity.

2011

Wildlife Wonderland

In 2011, the construction of our Wildlife Wonderland commenced. The habitats were carefully designed to mimic the natural environments of our animal residents, ensuring their well-being and happiness.

2013

Education & Awareness

In 2013, we launched our educational initiatives. We believe that understanding leads to appreciation, so we began educating visitors about the importance of wildlife conservation.

2016

A Dream Realized

In 2016, the dream of Wild Adventure Granada became a reality. We officially opened our gates to the public, inviting everyone to explore the wild like never before.

2020

Expanding Horizons

By 2020, our impact was expanding. We initiated global partnerships to support wildlife protection, ensuring our efforts reached beyond the park's borders.

Who We Are

Sarah Walker

Director

Sarah Walker, the dynamic director of Wild Adventure Granada, is a staunch advocate for wildlife conservation. Her journey into the heart of the natural world began in childhood, exploring the forests and meadows near her home. Raised by environmentally conscious parents, she developed an unwavering passion for wildlife and its protection.

Sarah's educational path led her to pursue a degree in wildlife biology. Her fieldwork took her to remote corners of the world, from the dense Amazon rainforest to the depths of the ocean. These experiences shaped her vision for Wild Adventure Granada, a sanctuary where people could connect with nature and actively participate in conservation.

With determination, Sarah turned her dream into reality. Collaborating with a passionate team, she oversaw the park's development, creating a harmonious space where wildlife thrives and visitors become stewards of the environment.

Beyond her directorial duties, Sarah remains hands-on in research and education, ensuring the park's mission is upheld. Her leadership inspires visitors to appreciate and protect the natural world.

Dr. Michael Roberts

Head Zoologist

Dr. Michael Roberts, our head zoologist, is a walking encyclopedia of animal knowledge. With a Ph.D. in zoology, he brings a wealth of experience to our park. His deep understanding of animal behavior and ecosystems has been instrumental in designing habitats that mimic the wild, ensuring our animal residents live happily and naturally.

Maria Gonzales

CONSERVATION MANAGER

Maria Gonzales is the guardian of our conservation initiatives. Her background in environmental science has led her to work tirelessly to protect endangered species and their habitats. She collaborates with local communities and organizations to create a network dedicated to wildlife preservation.

David Johnson

Wildlife Educator

David Johnson is our enthusiastic wildlife educator, known for making learning fun and engaging for all ages. He believes that understanding and appreciation of the natural world start with education. His captivating presentations and interactive programs inspire visitors to become advocates for conservation.

Dr. Emily White

Veterinary Specialist

Dr. Emily White, a dedicated veterinarian, ensures the well-being of our animal residents. With her expertise in veterinary medicine, she expertly oversees their medical care, ensuring their safety, health, and overall welfare. Her compassionate and diligent approach helps our animals feel secure, well-cared for, and thriving.

OUR TEAM WILD ADVENTURE

Working tirelessly behind the scenes, our dedicated team of rangers, caretakers, educators, and support staff plays a vital role in making Wild Adventure Granada a thriving haven for wildlife and a hub for environmental education.

Their unwavering commitment to our mission ensures that every day, we take strides toward a better, more sustainable world. It's their combined expertise, passion, and tireless dedication that make the magic of Wild Adventure Granada possible. We're grateful for each member's invaluable contribution.
