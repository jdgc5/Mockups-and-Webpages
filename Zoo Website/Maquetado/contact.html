Tickets and Prices

Reservations for advanced timed entry are mandatory for all visitors, including our valued members

Ticket prices fluctuate based on the day of your visit:

Adults & Teens | $16-26

Children (aged 2-12) | Enjoy a $4 discount

Children under 2 | Admission is free!

Parking: Secure parking for your vehicle is available at a rate of $8, and members enjoy complimentary parking privileges.

Stroller & Wheelchair Rentals: Enhance your visit's comfort with stroller and wheelchair rentals, starting at $11.

Group Discounts: Special discounts are offered for groups of 20 or more, making it an ideal choice for group outings and educational experiences.
